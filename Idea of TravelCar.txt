TravelCar, your future companion on every trip!

TravelCar is an app oriented to these people who wants to set up their trip, and be sure they are ready.
You will be able to know some tips and curious things. For example, check your oil level before a long trip, because I'm sure you do not want
any fault related with your car in the middle of your trip!
For those who have a limited budget, a fuel calculator will help them to know how much they will spend (approximately) in fuel.

And finally, if you want to do a trip but you do not have anything planned, there are some wonderful routes to drive in!

My idea was initially to design an app about cars because it's, probably, my passion just above computers (both are tech-related),
 and I thought would be useful something like this!
